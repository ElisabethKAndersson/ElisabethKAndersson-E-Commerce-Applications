Wednesday Walking Meetups

Come join us every Wednesday at 01.00 pm for a refreshing walk and socialization session outside the Senior Fitness facilities. This weekly gathering is specifically designed for seniors who are looking to stay active, meet new friends, and enjoy the outdoors. Whether you want to take a leisurely stroll or power walk, we welcome all fitness levels. Don't miss out on this fantastic opportunity to stay active and connect with others in a welcoming and inclusive environment. It is completely free to join!
